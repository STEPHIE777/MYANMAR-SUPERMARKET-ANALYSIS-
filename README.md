# MYANMAR-SUPERMARKET-ANALYSIS-: Myanmar Retail Insights (Q1 2019)

THESE ANALYSIS CAPTURES DETAILED TRANSACTION RECORD FROM WINMART SUPER MARKET  LOCATED IN MYANMAR, AND HAS BRANCHES ACROSS THREE CITIES: YANGON, NAYPYITAW AND MANDALAY.  IT SHOWS A COMPREHENSIVE VIEW OF SALES ACTIVITIES, CUSTOMER DEMOGRAPHICS AND PAYMENT METHOD FROM JANUARY TO MARCH 2019. 

 Executive Summary
This project provides a comprehensive data-driven analysis of WinMart Supermarket, a retail chain operating across Yangon, Naypyitaw, and Mandalay. By evaluating 1,000 transaction records from January to March 2019, I identified key sales drivers, seasonal fluctuations, and operational bottlenecks to provide actionable growth strategies for the chain.

Technical Stack

Advanced Excel: Data cleaning, Pivot Table generation, and KPI tracking.
MySQL: Complex querying for trend analysis and category performance.

 Detailed Findings & Analysis
 
1. Sales by Geography

Top Performer: Naypyitaw leads the region with $110,572 in sales.

Secondary Markets: Yangon ($106,203) and Mandalay ($106,200) show nearly identical performance, suggesting consistent but plateaued market penetration

2. Product Line Performance

Leaders: Food and Beverages ($56,154) and Sports and Travel ($55,126) are the primary revenue drivers.

Opportunity Area: Health and Beauty currently lags at $49,190, identifying a clear target for cross-selling initiatives.

3. Temporal Trends (The "Off-Peak" Challenge)

Peak Hours: Revenue spikes at 7 PM ($39,698) and 1 PM ($34,719).

Weak Windows: A significant drop is observed at 8 PM ($22,967) and 5 PM ($24,445), indicating missed opportunities during late-shift transitions.

4. Customer & Payment Metrics

Loyalty Impact: Members contributed $189,699, significantly outperforming normal customers at $133,276.

Payment Preference: Cash remains the dominant method at $112,202, followed by E-wallets ($109,999) and Credit Cards ($100,774).

Strategic Recommendations

Optimize Staffing: Implement "Early Bird" promotions to stimulate activity during low-traffic morning hours and adjust staffing for the 7 PM peak.

Cross-Selling Strategy: Bundle high-margin Food & Beverage items with underperforming Health & Beauty products to increase average transaction value.

Digital Transformation: Incentivize E-wallet and Credit Card use through digital loyalty rewards to reduce the logistical overhead of cash handling.
